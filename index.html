Get to Know me!

Hello! I'm Dominique Marcil, a passionate graphic design student with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in New Media from the University of Lethbridge. My journey in design is fueled by a deep commitment to creating visually compelling and inclusive digital experiences. With a diverse skill set that includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, responsive design, and UX/UI design, I focus on crafting solutions that are both aesthetically pleasing and cognitively efficient.
I believe that great design should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities. My work often centers on designing user interfaces that are intuitive and accommodating, with a particular emphasis on supporting neurodivergent individuals. I strive to create digital spaces that are safe, welcoming, and free from unnecessary cognitive load.
Beyond design, I'm also passionate about photography, particularly capturing the dynamic energy of snowboarding. My work is inspired by the natural beauty and adventure that define Alberta, and I enjoy blending these elements into my creative projects.
Whether I'm working on a website, rebranding a company, or exploring the latest in design trends, I approach every project with curiosity and a desire to make a meaningful impact.
